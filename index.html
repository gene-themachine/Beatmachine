A Simplified DAW for Beginners

This simulation provides beginners with a friendly experience of a DAW, also known as a digital audio workstation.

Making Your First Trap Beat

This simulation provides user with various instruments, including a bell, bass 808s, and drums- everything you need to build your first trap beat

This Simulation is Improving

The beta version allows users to experience what it's like to use a DAW and make trap beats. Further improvements are on the way so that users can experience a wide variety of instruments and features. There are a few bugs that needs correction as of now.
